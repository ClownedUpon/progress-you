# Progress You

A personal productivity notebook for people who want one place to manage their time, tasks, and notes — without the complexity of enterprise tools or the limitations of a basic to-do app.

Everything runs locally on your Windows machine. No accounts, no cloud, no subscriptions. Your data stays yours.

---

## What's inside

### Today
Your daily dashboard. See your schedule for the day alongside mini task boards for each section of your life. An Upcoming digest surfaces overdue and upcoming tasks so nothing with a deadline gets buried. Daily trackers appear here too — tick them off as you go.

### Timetable
A weekly planner with colour-coded time blocks, one column per day. Assign blocks to sections (work, study, personal projects, etc.) or mark them as breaks. Link tasks, notes, and trackers to any block. Save your schedule as a template and reapply it in one click.

### Taskboards
A kanban board per section with three columns: Backlog, This Week, and Done. Drag tasks between columns, set due dates and priorities, attach checklists, and use spacer dividers to organise long lists. Completed tasks can be archived to keep boards clean — they stay accessible in the Log and through search.

### Notes
A rich-text notebook per section. Notes are organised in a collapsible tree — nest sub-notes under any note, like folders. Each note has a full editor with font, size, colour, formatting, and bullet lists. Link notes to tasks and trackers so everything stays connected.

### Trackers
Build habits and track routines. Create trackers with custom active days (e.g. weekdays only), tick them off daily, and watch your streak grow. Each tracker has a weekly grid, monthly calendar, and streak counter. Pin tasks and notes to a tracker to keep related work in one place.

### Calendar
A monthly calendar view showing all tasks with due dates. Click any day to see what's scheduled.

### Log
A monthly archive of everything you've completed, grouped by section. A record of what you've actually done.

### Stats
A summary of your week: hours scheduled, tasks active and completed, time allocation by section, completion rates, and tracker consistency.

## Features that work everywhere

- **Global search (Ctrl+K)** — Find any task, note, timetable block, or tracker instantly. Supports regex for power users.
- **Quick Capture (Ctrl+Space)** — Jot down a task or note from anywhere in the app without navigating away.
- **Pin Overlay** — A floating dashboard you can open from any view showing today's schedule, active tasks, and daily trackers.
- **Undo toasts** — Accidentally delete or archive something? A 5-second undo toast appears at the bottom of the screen.
- **Sections** — Organise everything by areas of your life. Each section has its own colour, taskboard, notebook, and timetable blocks. Rename, recolour, or remove them anytime.
- **Interconnectivity** — Tasks, notes, trackers, and timetable blocks all link to each other. Every item is reachable from every relevant context.

---

## Your data, your rules

- All data is stored locally on your machine — nothing is sent to any server.
- **Automatic backups** run every 24 hours (configurable). The 10 most recent backups are kept.
- **Export** your entire notebook as a JSON file at any time. **Import** it back on a fresh install or a different machine.
- No account required. No internet connection required after installation.
